Smart Home Features

Smart Lighting

Smart lights can be a huge convenience when you don't want to get off the couch or when you've just gotten comfortable in bed. Their price falls around $50 to implement.

Cleaning Robots

Cleaning robots such as Roombas can make keeping dirt, dust, and fur off your floors easy. These are especially useful for elderly or disabled persons because they can clean in hard to get areas. Depending on their quailty they most reliable ones cost between $100-$2000.

Thermostat Control

Smart thermostats can save time an money as you come home to a warm or cool house without leaveing anything unnessary on to waste your money. This can be done from your couch or your office. And combined with a smart timer you can wake up to a warmed room. This system needs professional installation which can cost from $200 to $1000.

Smart Cameras

Smart cameras allow the user to monitor their homes remotely or detect trouble when you are home. This can let home owners rest easy and safely. The cost of the camera itself will come to around $200 then operating it will cost about $20/month.

Smart Lock

Smart locks can save the trouble of dragging out your keys, taking time out of your day to be home to let someone like a repair man in, or allowing guests to enter without stopping what you're doing. Some even come with voice control and tamper alarms. They can be aquired for between $100 and $300.

Smart Washing Machine

Costing from $750-$1000 a smart washing machine will remove the doubt of did I remember to press the on button? Is there wet luandry sitting in there?
